Rooted in Nature

Awildroux is more than a brand—it's a lifestyle grown from the soil of intention and connection. Rooted deep in the wilderness, I forage, craft, and create using what nature offers freely. From wild seeds and botanicals to hand-burned art and herbal balms, everything is crafted with purpose and care. Whether you're seeking holistic healing, seasonal wisdom, or a slower, earth-centered life, you're welcome here.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Wild Box is a subscription service that delivers seasonal, handcrafted items from nature straight to your door. You can choose between monthly, quarterly, or annual subscriptions. Boxes are shipped on the 15th of each month, and you can cancel or modify your subscription at any time.

Absolutely! Wild Box subscriptions make wonderful gifts. During checkout, you can select the "This is a gift" option and provide the recipient's shipping information. We also offer gift cards that can be applied toward any subscription or product in our shop.

You can manage your subscription at any time by logging into your account on our website. From there, you can change your subscription type, update your shipping address, or cancel your subscription.
